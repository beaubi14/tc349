Final Project: My Design

For my final project, I would like to create a portfolio website for my Artwork. The goal of my website that would showcase my 2D and 3D art and my web and game design skills. This site would also provide the necessary background information and to allow an outlet for potential employers to have access to my resume. My site will also show some of the games that I had made at the Game Jam that I helped make during my time here at MSU.
With this idea in mind, I performed the card sorting exercise with the same participants that I used for the previous card sorting exercise on the NASA Moon site. The result were extraordinarily similar, in the sense that most people wanted to group the Resume, About Me, and My Education sections together, and the art categories were generally grouped as well. It was common for the Game Jam Games and Web Design and Maintenance sections to be either in their own group or grouped with Social Media Link and Sites of Interest (likely due to a misinterpretation of the subject).

Card Sorting Data for My Site: Me

For organizing the information for my final project, I found that it would be easier to make three main categories. One would include all the art that I had worked on, one would be for my about information and a link to my resume, and the last would be contact info/my social media links.
