My Portfolio

This is my portfolio section of my site. This is where I keep all my different types of artwork and game dev work. My main interest is to be a 3D character artist, but I have dabbled in 2D game development, and have lots of concept artwork. Check out my work, and feel free to contact me for more information.

3D Work

    Here is the section where I keep my 3D art work. This includes my high poly work, and my low poly work. I have proficiency in Autodesk: Maya and Pixologic: Zbrush, but I am alway looking for new methods and for making my artwork. I am still working on developing my style. Look forward to more artwork in the future.

Electronic 2D Work

This section is my 2D Electric Gallery. The work flow that I use for developing most of my electronic 2D art is by first sketching it out, then scanning my sketch into either photoshop or illustrator (depending on the art style I'm working for). I have made 2D game assets for Javascript games and unity games. I will be posting more 2D art in the future.

Sketches

This section is my sketch gallery, which houses my sketch art and concept art work. In many cases, I do some of my best work on pencil and paper, and I find that I have much better results, after I have practiced a characters design several times in traditional medium. Check out some of the concepts that I have made and look for more of them in the future.

Javascript Games

This section is where I keep my Java Games that I have been making here at MSU. Here are some alpha state games that I have worked on, just for learning the program. Try out the games see how you like them. I made a platformer where I wanted to work on learning out to make killable enemies, and gravity and acceleration on the player. The second alpha I made was a simple endless runner. This game has far less polish, but it helps demonstrates the general dynamic of the game.
