Welcome!

This is the personal website of Joseph Leroy Beaubien Jr, I am a recent Michigan State University(MSU) graduate. I have worked most of my college career as an information technology worker, working first as a student consultant at Jackson College, and then working for Media Services under the Health Information Technology department at MSU. This website is a serves as a portfolio to help showcase my work, and communicate with interested parties. Feel free to look at my work and give me feedback.
